# Landotron Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 June 2026

Landotron (the "App") is designed to help users view aerodrome landing fee information and calculate payable amounts.

## Summary

- We do not collect personal data.
- We do not track users across apps or websites.
- We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.
- We do not require account creation.

## Information We Collect

Landotron does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

The fee values you enter (for example aircraft weight and persons onboard) are processed locally on your device only to calculate totals shown in the App.

## Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not share personal data with third parties.

## Third-Party Links

The App may display links to external aerodrome/operator websites for fee references. If you open those links, you leave the App and those websites have their own privacy practices and policies.

## Payments

Landotron currently does not process in-app payments for listed aerodromes where payment is disabled. The App may display bank transfer details provided by operators.

## Children’s Privacy

Landotron does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

## Security

Since Landotron does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no backend personal data store managed by us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
